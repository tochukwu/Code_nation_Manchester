1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

    The topic itself is multifaceted, no business would not be able to function in the modern age without using some form of IT or the other, from implementing predictive analysis software on networked farm machinery such as plows to tractors to preemptively diagnose issues with the machinery before they even develop and become an issue or interrupt/holt farming activities . To the automated voice system used in customer service to help direct customer(s) request, enquiries or complaints to the right department in order to better resolve it, or self-driving trucks for the trucking and logistics sector. The uses and implementation of IT are limitless and ubiquitous however the uses of IT in business broadly come under:

    Communication

    As we all know communication is paramount in all aspects of life, no different in the business world. Communications between each department and levels of management insures all the employees, team leads and management are in-sync and cohesively working towards a common goal. Each business choses the type of communication applications and platforms that they work with. As of recent a cloud based solution known as Service Now and is gaining notoriety. The solutions is cross departmental which means the company's an entire which encourages openness and better accountability

    Decision Making

    Due to the current climate and the fast-paced nature of trades and business itself, it is ever more paramount that Information Technology systems are elicited in order to facilitate more nimble business decisions without loose of accuray. A decade ago systems like CRMs and other database management systems were considered cutting edge now Big data, Google Analytics, AWS and other Decision Engines are used to give berter foresight in order to aid the human agents make a decision
